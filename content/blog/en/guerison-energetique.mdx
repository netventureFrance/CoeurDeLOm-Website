# Energy healing: a path to balance

Energy healing works on subtle blockages that affect our well-being. Discover how these practices can help you.

## What is vital energy?

Vital energy circulates in our body through subtle channels. When it is blocked, it can create physical and emotional imbalances.

## Different approaches

### Reiki
Reiki is a Japanese healing technique through laying on of hands that channels universal energy.

### Magnetism
Magnetism uses the practitioner's energy to rebalance the patient's energy field.

### Chromotherapy
Chromotherapy uses colors and their vibrations to harmonize energy centers.

## Benefits of energy therapies

- Reduction of stress and tension
- Strengthening of the immune system
- Harmonization of emotions
- Better sleep
- Mental clarity

## For whom?

Energy therapies are for everyone, as a complement to traditional medicine.

## Conclusion

Energy balance is essential for our overall well-being. These gentle practices offer valuable support on the path of healing.
